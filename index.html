02

Craft — Color · Material · Finish

I bring a CMF sensibility to digital products — treating color, material and finish as deliberately on screen as a designer would on a physical object.

C — Color

Systemized palettes & tokens

Semantic, token-driven color that holds up across light, dark, exam and practice modes — and stays consistent from web to app.

M — Material

Surfaces with depth

Frosted glass, grain and layered translucency, plus fully specified component states — interfaces with a material you can almost feel.

F — Finish

The last 10%

Motion easing, micro-interactions, optical spacing and pixel polish — the finish that makes a product feel resolved, not just built.
