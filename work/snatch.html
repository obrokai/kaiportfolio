Snatch — a complete fitness coaching platform

A mobile + web product that turns a coach's spare minutes into bookable, on-demand video consultations — designed end-to-end from 0 to 1.

Role
Lead Product Designer & Strategist · Sole designer
Timeline
2024 – 2025
Platform
iOS App & Web
Scope
Research, Branding, UI, Design System, Prototype, Strategy
The Problem

The fitness industry relied on fragmented scheduling and private messaging — creating unstable income for coaches and inconsistent experiences for students. The traditional model couldn't accommodate flexible time slots, nor address the quick, urgent micro-questions students had between sessions.

The Solution

Snatch introduced flexible 5-, 10- and 15-minute video consultations — letting coaches monetize the gaps between sessions, while students receive immediate, personalized guidance.

The approach emphasized strategy-driven growth and systematic design: a token-based design system for cross-platform consistency, streamlined conversational flows, and simplified administrative operations for coaches.
